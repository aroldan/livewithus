Poll Types

What's the difference between single vote and excellence polls?

Single vote polls allow users to vote for one and only one option, while excellence polls let users vote for as many options as they think are "excellent." This can be useful in building consensus when different people in a group are all okay with multiple options but have varying preferences.

Financial Snapshot

What is a financial snapshot?

At livewith.us we treat the house almost like a bank. Everytime you buy something at the store that other people in your house are accountable for like toilet paper or dinner you are in effect loaning the house money. Whenever you are included in a share of the price of something you are borrowing money from the house. In order to help you better understand where you stand in this debt/loan relationship we've created the financial snapshot graph.

What does the financial snapshot graph mean?

In the financial snapshot graph you'll note that the positive side and the negative side sum to zero. That's because the house itself cannot be positive or negative. Each member of the house is listed either in the positive or negative column depending if they owe money or are owed money. The length of the bar represents how much money you owe or are owed.
